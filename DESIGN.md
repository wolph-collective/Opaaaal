# Design System

## Visual Design Rules for Stitch
*Use these rules when generating screens.*

### Color Palette
- **Primary Action (Success/Go):** #ADFFA6 (Mint Green)
- **Secondary Action (Calm/Info):** #B0A8FE (Melrose Purple)
- **Background:** #040404 (Deep Black)
- **Surface/Cards:** #121212 (Dark Gray, slightly lighter than bg)
- **Text Primary:** #FFFFFF (White)
- **Text Secondary:** #A1A1AA (Neutral Gray)
- **Border/Divider:** #27272A (Subtle Gray)

### Typography
- **Headings:** Elegant Serif (like 'Opal Script' vibes) or Clean Geometric Sans-Serif (Inter/SF Pro). Use Sans-Serif for readability in UI.
- **Body:** Clean Sans-Serif, high legibility.
- **Weights:** Light to Regular for body, Bold for key data points.

### Components
- **Buttons:**
    - *Primary:* Pill-shaped (Full rounded), Mint Green background, Black text.
    - *Secondary:* Pill-shaped, Transparent with White border or Dark Gray background.
- **Cards:**
    - Dark Gray (#121212) background.
    - Rounded corners (24px - large, friendly curves).
    - Subtle or no inner borders.
    - Glassmorphism effects (blur) allowed for "gem-like" feel.
- **Icons:** Minimalist, thin stroke, white or mint green.

### Layout
- **Mobile-First:** Single column, comfortable touch targets (44px+).
- **Spacing:** Generous breathing room (24px, 32px margins).
- **vibe:** "Cozy Clarity". No clutter.
